Career Ideas

In the way of future career plans, my thoughts have maintained a vagueness that, as I look at my classmates, fits most other seniors. I have many passions, and I could see myself doing a lot of different jobs. One of the most likely trajectories that I predict is going into the computer science field in the realm of software development. A good saying that, I think, mirrors my path is, "Shoot for the moon, because even if you miss, you'll land among the stars."
